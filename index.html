About Me

I was Born in Johannesburg, South Africa in 2002.
I grew up there and went to school there. After finishing high school I moved to Cape Town to stay with my grandmother, while there I took a sailing course and got my YachtMasther's skipper license with the plan of working overseas and doing a few charters and maybe some deliveries.
I decided to put that on hold in the mean while to try and gain some extra skills in case sailing wouldn't work out.
I had always been interested in the latest and greatest of technology and so it was only natural I was drawn to coding and developing.
I started with Python and progressed quickly. However my heart became set on different things and I missed the visual design aspect of building programes.
A few articles and experimenting later and here we are, I love CSS and my previous experience with Python helped with the thinking and manouvering behind JavaScript.
So far I have enjoyed the journey and only hope it doesn't end.
